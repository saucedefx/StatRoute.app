Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

Introduction

StatRoute.app ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our fantasy football recommendation service.

Information We Collect

When you use StatRoute.app, we collect:

  • Yahoo Fantasy Football Data: Your league information, team roster, player statistics, and matchup data
  • Authentication Information: OAuth tokens to securely access your Yahoo Fantasy account
  • Usage Data: How you interact with our service to improve recommendations

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect solely for the following purposes:

  • Generating AI-powered start/sit recommendations for your fantasy football team
  • Analyzing player performance and matchup data
  • Improving our recommendation algorithms
  • Providing you with insights about your fantasy team

Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not share, sell, or distribute your Yahoo Fantasy data with any third parties. Your data is used exclusively to provide you with recommendations and improve our service.

We may use third-party services for infrastructure (hosting, analytics) but these services do not have access to your Yahoo Fantasy data.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • Encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Secure OAuth token storage
  • Limited data retention
  • Regular security audits

Your Rights and Control

You have complete control over your data:

  • Disconnect at Any Time: You can revoke StatRoute.app's access to your Yahoo Fantasy account at any time
  • Data Deletion: You can request deletion of your data by disconnecting your account
  • Access Your Data: You can request a copy of the data we store about you

Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide our service. When you disconnect your account, we will delete your personal data within 30 days.

Third-Party Services

StatRoute.app integrates with Yahoo Fantasy Sports. Your use of Yahoo services is governed by Yahoo's own privacy policy and terms of service.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please contact us.
